## üéØ The Niche Selection Formula

### Step 1: Follow the Money
Look for:
- üîç Active Google Ads in the space
- üí∞ Existing expensive tools/services
- üìà Growing search trends
- üè¢ B2B potential

### Step 2: Find the Pain
Ask:
- What takes too long?
- What costs too much?
- What's too complicated?
- What breaks often?

### Step 3: Validate Demand
Test with:
- Reddit communities (are they complaining?)
- Facebook groups (are they asking for solutions?)
- Twitter searches (are they willing to pay?)
- Google Trends (is interest growing?)
