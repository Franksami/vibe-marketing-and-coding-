## üí∞ Pricing Strategy Mastery

### The 10x Value Rule
Your price should be 1/10th of the value you create:
- Save client 20 hours/month @ $50/hour = $1,000/month value
- Your price: $100-200/month retainer

### Psychology Pricing Tactics

#### 1. Charm Pricing
- $297 instead of $300
- $1,497 instead of $1,500
- $4,997 instead of $5,000

#### 2. Anchor High
Always show your premium package first:
- Premium: $4,997 ‚¨Ö (anchor)
- Standard: $1,997 (seems reasonable)
- Basic: $497 (bargain)

#### 3. Bundle Value
Don't list features, stack value:
- Custom AI Assistant ($2,000 value)
- ‚ûï Team Training ($500 value)
- ‚ûï 30-Day Support ($500 value)
- ‚ûï Monthly Optimization ($300 value)
- = Total Value: $3,300
- **Your Investment: $1,497** (Save $1,803!)

## üö´ Scope Creep Prevention

### Clear Boundaries in Every Package

```markdown
‚úÖ INCLUDED:
- [Specific deliverable 1]
- [Specific deliverable 2]
- [Specific number] revisions
- [Specific timeframe] support

‚ùå NOT INCLUDED:
- Additional features beyond scope
- Unlimited revisions
- Work outside business hours
- Third-party integration costs
```

### The Change Order System
"I'd be happy to add that! Here's a change order for the additional work:"
- New feature: $XXX
- Timeline impact: +X days
- Total project adjustment: $XXX
