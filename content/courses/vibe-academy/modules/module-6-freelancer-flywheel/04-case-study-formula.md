## üé¨ Video Case Studies

### The 3-Minute Formula

**0:00-0:15** - Hook with shocking result
"How we cut response time from 6 hours to 2 minutes"

**0:15-0:45** - Problem overview with emotion
Show the chaos, stress, lost revenue

**0:45-1:45** - Solution walkthrough
Screen recording of actual system

**1:45-2:30** - Results with numbers
Animated graphs showing transformation

**2:30-3:00** - Client testimonial + CTA
Real human confirming the impact

### Video Production Tips

- Use Loom or Vidyard for easy recording
- Add captions (85% watch muted)
- Include your face (builds trust)
- Use annotations to highlight key points
- Keep energy high throughout

## üéØ Metrics That Matter

### Always Quantify These

1. **Time Saved**
   - Hours per week/month
   - Percentage reduction
   - Tasks automated

2. **Money Saved/Earned**
   - Direct cost reduction
   - Revenue increase
   - ROI percentage

3. **Efficiency Gains**
   - Process speed increase
   - Error rate reduction
   - Throughput improvement

4. **User Satisfaction**
   - NPS improvement
   - Support ratings
   - Adoption rates

### The Magic Formula
```
[Specific Number] + [Metric] + [Timeframe] = Compelling Result

Examples:
```

## üí° Pro Portfolio Tips

7. **SEO Optimize**: Use keywords in case study titles
