### Email Personalization Checklist

- [ ] Reference recent company news
- [ ] Mention specific metric/problem
- [ ] Include competitor comparison
- [ ] Attach actual value (video/audit)
- [ ] Show you understand their industry
- [ ] Keep under 150 words
- [ ] One clear CTA

### Subject Lines That Get Opens

**Formula**: [Specific Issue] + [Company Name] + [Intrigue]

Examples:
- "TechCorp's 6-hour response time (2-min fix inside)"
- "Why Competitor X beats AcmeCo at conversions"
- "Quick AI audit for [Company] - 3 opportunities found"
- "[Mutual Connection] suggested I reach out"
- "Question about [Company]'s Q3 automation goals"
