## Segmentation Rules

### Engagement Segments
- **Hot**: Opened all emails → Fast track to sale
- **Warm**: Opened 2-3 emails → Continue nurture
- **Cold**: Opened 0-1 emails → Re-engagement campaign

### Action-Based Segments
- Clicked but didn't buy → Objection handling
- Downloaded resource → Topic-specific follow-up
- Attended webinar → Special offer
